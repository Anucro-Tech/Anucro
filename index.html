Why it’s different

Compared to modular systems from Dymax, LOCTITE, and Nordson, Anucro eliminates separate cure lights, foot pedals, and extra controllers—reducing cost, footprint, and setup time.

All‑in‑one workflow

Dispense and cure in a single unit. Arrives ready to run—no add‑ons required.

No pneumatics

Servo‑driven positive displacement. Fewer utilities, fewer failure points.

Dual‑operator

Two independent channels increase throughput without extra footprint.

Fast UV‑LED cure

1–15 s typical at 365 / 385 / 405 nm (capable 365–420 nm).

Maintenance‑free

No air lines or valves. Few moving parts and simple upkeep.

Bench‑top footprint

Lightweight form factor with customizable fixture layout.

Technical Specs

Dispensing

  • Servo‑driven linear actuator with feedback
  • Positive‑displacement control (no shop air)
  • Dual independent channels
  • Sensor feedback

Curing

  • UV‑LED COBs at 365 / 385 / 405 nm (capable 365–420 nm)
  • Typical cure time: 1–15 s
  • Customizable fixturing for optimal light placement
  • PWM dimming control

System

  • Single integrated controller for dispense + cure
  • Maintenance‑free design
  • Bench‑top, lightweight chassis
  • Plug‑and‑play (standard mains power)
